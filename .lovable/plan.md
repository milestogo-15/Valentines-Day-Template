## Design Theme

**Visual Style**: Vintage scrapbook with aged paper textures, warm cream and dusty rose colors, handwritten-style headings, and polaroid photo frames.

**Interactive Element**: Single page-flip animation when clicking "Next" - like turning pages in a real scrapbook album.

**Animation Style**: Playful and animated with floating hearts, bouncing elements, and lively interactions throughout.
